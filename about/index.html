About Victor

Hello, my name is Victor Lawrence. I have a BFA from Columbus State University in Theatrical Design and Technology and I like building stuff for the web. I'm an Apprentice Software Engineer for a startup whose headquarters is located in New Haven, Connecticut. I help build new and helpful interfaces for our customers and employees to use.

I am passionate about making things feel smooth, quick and intuitive. In other words, I want to build interfaces so good that you don't really notice them.

Want to make something together? I'm game. You can find all the best ways to contact me here.
